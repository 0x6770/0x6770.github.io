# Why ions pass through cell surface membrane using facilitated diffusion or active transport

  • ions are charged particles / polar / hydrophilic
  • cannot pass hydrophobic interior of phospholipid bilayer
  • so must pass through transport proteins / carrier proteins / channel proteins (facilitated diffusion)

# Characteristics of water

  • Strong cohesion (between water molecules) / adhesion (between water molecules and wall of xylem) -> continous flow
  • polar molecule / have dipoles -> good solvent
  • form hydrogen bonding
  • high surface tension
  • low viscosity
  • high latent heat of vaporisation (take lots of energy when evaporate from surface)
  • high specific heat capacity -> keep body temperature stable
  • pH7/neutral
